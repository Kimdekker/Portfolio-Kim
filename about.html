Who am I

Hello, my name is
Kim Dekker

I’m a creative developer and digital designer from Alkmaar, the Netherlands. In 2022 I graduated from the MediaCollege in Amsterdam and got my degree in Visual Design with an expertise in digital media.

I’ve been studying further at the University of Applied Sciences and currently doing my BaSc in Communication and Multimedia Design.

I also like helping students with code as a student assistant and enjoy to work with my own clients in free time.

About my skills

Skills I own

I would describe myself as a person that is eager to learn and has interest at everything that that gets thrown in their way.

I'm not scared for challenges and always want to complete those. I'll do everything in my power to get where I want, without spending to much time waisting. I'd call it a swirl of discipline and motivation that keeps me going.

I'm a good communicator as well. I'd like to know stuff and ask questions when needed, what makes me a enjoyable team-player.

My style would be a progressive designer/developer that tries to push the boundries if it comes to possibilities, whether I work on tech or design topics.

I like working with people, for people.

Passion

Passion

As typical as it may sounds, I truly love what I'm doing. It's all one big hobby for me.

I like trying new things out, tinkering with stuff, keeping my head buzy with new trends around design and tech.

Where I get excited about, is making stuff really cool as well as making things work as they should. I don't like making stuff just work, without the "wow, that's cool" element.

Things I enjoy spendinng time on

Outside of being dedicated to design/development, I enjoy spending time with friends and family. I believe it's important to maintain these kind of relationships, so I will make time for those people as much as I want.

Going travelling is something I really love doing. It gives my mind oxygen and new insights about the world we live in. Exploring new grounds is something I believes help me being good at what I do.

During the week, I enjoy playing rugby. Rugby has been my thing since I was young, playing international tournaments with the Dutch rugby team as well. I still play on high levels, but not as a professional anymore. Rugby made the person that I am today.

Furthermore, I am a music lover. Music keeps me going, focussed, motivated and more. whether we're talking about punk, ska or classic film music. I like to own records too.
